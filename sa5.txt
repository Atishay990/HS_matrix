CAPITALISM (LAISSEZ-FAIRE)
 • Individuals own the means of production.
 • Based on competition, the owners determine production and set prices.
 • The pursuit of profit is the reason for distributing goods and services.
 SOCIALISM
 • The public owns the means of production.
 • Central committees plan production and set prices; no competition.
 • No profit motive in the distribution of goods and services.
